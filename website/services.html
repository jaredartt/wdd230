Amenities & Services

Free Grab & Go Breakfast

With us, you will be spoiled! Our guests rave about our free grab-and-go breakfast, which includes pastries, protein, fruit, coffee, tea, and more. Start your day with our delicious breakfast and start an exciting day exploring Nashville!

Entertainment & Recreation

Our guests can enjoy a variety of relaxation and entertainment options in our comfortable hotel. When you're not home, you can take a dip in our outdoor pool or work out in our fitness center. The fitness center offers stationary bikes, free weights and treadmills. In our fitness room, you'll also find a large flat-screen TV so you can watch your favorite shows while you work out.

Guest Conveniences

You'll feel right at home while staying with us, our excellent service and convenient amenities add an extra value to your stay. That’s why we offer free WiFi from the comfort of your room as well as throughout the hotel. Business travelers will appreciate our convenient business center with two computers with print and copy services.

Pet Policy

Now you can bring your favorite pet with you, we're a pet-friendly inn!We charge a nominal pet fee of $20.00 per pet/per night. There is a maximum weight limit of 20 lbs with up to 2 pets per room.
