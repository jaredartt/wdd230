Wedding Reception

Continue the celebrations when you book one of these beautiful wedding venues with accommodation. A slap-up breakfast with your nearest and dearest is the only way to get over all the emotions!

An unforgettable day

Flooded with natural light, our wedding receptions are one of the most beautiful venues to marry. This eco-friendly, former hunting pavilion is set right on the back of each Inn across the world; in summer, fling the doors wide open and you'll feel like you're eating your reception meal among the beautiful grounds.

It is licensed for civil ceremonies and offers an idyllic setting for your special day. The orangery is light and airy, and with the doors open, it's like being seated in the gardens. Purpose-built for hosting bespoke weddings year-round, the orangery offers a flexible, contemporary, stylish, and eco-friendly venue ideal for modern weddings. The Pavilion provides a scenic setting for an outdoor celebration, immersed in the picturesque grounds. Glass walls allow for panoramic views of the gorgeous estate, filling the space with natural light.
