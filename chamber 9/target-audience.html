Target Audience.

Our target audience are focused on people who run their own businesses (like freelancers) living Chiclana de la Frontera, a city in the south of Spain.

Example personas.

Example 1

Name: Lola Martínez

Age: 41

Business: English Academy

Description: Lola lives in the suburbs of Chiclana and is happily married with 3 kids, but one salary is not enough for her family, so she decides to open an English Academy in the city center. She has never done any business start up, so she needs this Chamber of Commerce in Chiclana so she can properly prepare her start up. One of the cornerstones of the Chamber of Commerce in Chiclana is the development of entrepreneurial spirit and fostering the creation of new businesses.

Example 2

Name: Mario Román

Age: 28

Business: Restaurant

Description: Mario owns a restuarant in the coast of Chiclana, where there are a lot of the typical southern Spanish restaurants like his. They all sell pretty similar food, so it's really difficult to stand out among the others, he needs help with making an inflection point in his restaurnt by innovating and improving his competitive position in the market. That's what this Chamber of Commerce in Chiclana is for, to offer him our services, help and support in administrative processes to make his journey easier.
